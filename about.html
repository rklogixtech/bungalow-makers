About Bungalow Makers

Leading architectural excellence since 2016

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Bungalow Makers began with a simple vision: to transform the way people design and build their dream homes. What started as a small architectural firm in Udaipur has grown into one of India's most trusted home design companies.

With over 8+ years of experience, we've completed more than 7000+ projects across residential and commercial sectors. Our journey has been guided by innovation, client satisfaction, and architectural excellence.

We believe every home tells a story, and we're here to help you write yours.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable, and client-centric architectural solutions that transform living spaces while maintaining the highest standards of quality and affordability.

Vision

To become India's most preferred architectural design firm, recognized for excellence in creating homes that inspire and communities that thrive.

Values

Integrity, Innovation, Client Satisfaction, Sustainability, and Excellence in every project we undertake.
